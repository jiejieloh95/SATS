Sick leave and hospitalisation leave are deducted only for the employee's working days. It excludes non-working days, rest days and public holidays.

This is because the employee is not expected to report for work on a non-working day, rest day or public holiday. Hence there is no need for him to apply for sick leave or hospitalization leave to be absent from work.

NOTE: If an employee falls sick during his annual leave, his absence from work would still be treated as annual leave and not sick leave.
