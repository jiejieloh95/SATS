You are eligible for re-employment if you:

- Are a Singapore citizen or Singapore permanent resident.

- Have served your current employer for at least 3 years before turning 62.

- Have satisfactory work performance, as assessed by the employer.

- Are medically fit to continue working.

P/S: Employees are considered medically fit to continue working as long as their health does not affect their job performance. Employees can only be deemed unfit to work once a proper medical assessment has been made by a doctor. Satisfactory work performance is one of the criteria for re-employment. If you're assessing an employee's eligibility for re-employment, you can take into account their performance over the last 2 to 3 years.
