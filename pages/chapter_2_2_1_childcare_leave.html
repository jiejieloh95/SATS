Employees who have twins, or multiple children do not get additional childcare leave entitlement. The employees should reach an agreement with their supervisors on the days that they are taking childcare leave while being mindful of work schedules and timelines.

P.S.: An employee is entitled to 6 days of paid childcare leave per year if his / her child is a Singapore citizen. If his/her child is not a Singapore citizen, the paid childcare leave entitlement is 2 days per year.
